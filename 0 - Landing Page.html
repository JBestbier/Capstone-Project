Intel Rocket Lake - The newest from intel.

Intel confirmed in 2021 that they will be blessing our computers with it's 11th Gen Rocket Lake processor, with PCIe 4.0 support on motherboards.

This just in time to compete with AMD's next-gen Zen 3 architecture processors. Get all the details here
Nividia is removing these drivers for old GPU's.

As all things, the best has come to it's end. This was confirmed on 23 May 2021 by Nividia that they will stop pushing updates for the GTX600 and 700 series GPU's.

"The incoming R470 graphics driver will be the last to support some of the firm's older graphics cards", said Darren Allan from techradar.com. Read all about it here
